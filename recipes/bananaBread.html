Banana Bread

Description

This banana bread recipe creates the most delicious, moist loaf with loads of banana flavor. Why compromise the banana flavor? Friends and family love this recipe and say it's by far the best! It tastes wonderful toasted. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Flour: All-purpose flour gives the banana bread structure. 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • Baking soda: Baking soda acts as a leavener, which means it helps the banana bread rise. 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • Salt: A pinch of salt enhances the overall flavor, but it won't make the loaf taste salty. 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • Butter: A stick of butter lends richness, moisture, and irresistible flavor. 1/2 cup butter
  • Banana Of course, you'll need bananas! Choose overripe bananas. 2 1/3 cups mashed overriped bananas
  • Eggs: Eggs act as a binding agent, which means they hold the batter together. 2 large eggs, beaten
  • Brown sugar: Brown sugar sweetens things up and adds a hint of warmth.3/4 cup brown sugar

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x5-inch loaf pan.
  2. Combine flour, baking soda, and salt in a large bowl. Beat brown sugar and butter with an electric mixer in a separate large bowl until smooth. Stir in eggs and mashed bananas until well blended. Stir banana mixture into flour mixture until just combined. Pour batter into the prepared loaf pan.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 60 minutes. Let bread cool in pan for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack to cool completely.
